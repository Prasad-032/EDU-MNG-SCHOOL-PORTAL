Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

First, make sure you're selecting the correct role (Admin, Teacher, Student, or Parent) on the login page. Then try the "Forgot Password" link to reset your password. If you still can't log in, contact your school's admin — they can reset your account. If your school isn't on EduManage yet, use the form above.

Accounts for students, teachers and parents are created by your school's administrator. Contact your school's principal or admin office and ask them to add you to EduManage. They'll send you a login link via email or SMS.

The school's principal or administrator can register at edumanage.app/register. It takes about 10 minutes to set up and is free for the first 30 days. No credit card required.

Go to the login page and click "Forgot password?" below the password field. Enter your registered email and we'll send you a reset link within 2 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

Payment updates can take up to 30 minutes to reflect. If it's been longer, please contact us using the form above and attach a screenshot of your payment confirmation. We'll resolve it within 2 hours.
